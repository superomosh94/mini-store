Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind MiniStore, where quality meets style and customer satisfaction is our top priority.

Who We Are

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2015, MiniStore started as a small boutique in downtown New York with a simple mission: to provide high-quality products with exceptional customer service. What began as a single storefront has now grown into a beloved brand with customers across the country.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, started MiniStore with just 50 products in inventory. Today, we offer over 5,000 carefully curated items that reflect our commitment to quality, sustainability, and timeless design.

Our Growth Journey

Over the years, we've expanded both our physical presence and our online platform, but we've never lost sight of our core values. Each product in our store is still hand-selected by our team to ensure it meets our high standards.

In 2020, we launched our e-commerce platform, allowing us to serve customers nationwide while maintaining the personal touch we're known for. Our customer service team averages 5+ years with the company, ensuring you get knowledgeable, friendly assistance.

Our Core Values

Quality First

We meticulously vet every product to ensure it meets our durability, functionality, and design standards before it reaches you.

Customer Love

Your satisfaction is our priority. Our team goes above and beyond to create exceptional shopping experiences.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices, from sourcing to packaging, to reduce our environmental impact.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our product selection and services to meet your changing needs and preferences.
