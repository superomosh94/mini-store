Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your customer service hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST, and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags attached. Some exclusions apply - please see our full return policy for details.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please check our international shipping page for more details.

How can I change or cancel my order?

If your order hasn't been processed for shipping yet, you may be able to change or cancel it. Please contact our customer service team immediately with your order number.
